# Choosing tone

Tone should always adapt based on context.

---

Choosing the right tone is especially critical when writing product content. These guidelines will help you decide what tone is appropriate for a given situation.

## Positive situations

Use a casual and enthusiastic tone. Be encouraging and offer users a next step where appropriate. Be careful about empty moments of celebration.

Be careful with humor, it can be hard to convey. Test your content with actual users to see how they respond, and when in doubt, keep a straight face.
