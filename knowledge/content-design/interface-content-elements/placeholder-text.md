## Placeholder text

- Placeholder text (or ghost text) should provide additional guidance, context, or examples. It should never be the only context userâ€™s are provided with. Field labels should be descriptive enough to guide the users even without additional context of the placeholder text.

- Placeholder text *does not* have to be used in every form field. If it is redundant with the label, remove it. Otherwise we risk training users to ignore our content.

- Placeholder text is not good for accessibility and adds to cognitive load due to it disappearing when you click in the field and the low contrast of the text.

- Add formatting to the label or under the field if applicable (&ast;to be reviewed with the form field component).

- Placeholder text should be minimal.
