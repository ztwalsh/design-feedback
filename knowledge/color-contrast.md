# Color & Contrast

## Color Palette
- Limit primary colors to 2-3 maximum
- Use accent colors sparingly for calls-to-action
- Maintain consistency across the entire interface
- Consider color psychology and brand alignment

## Contrast Requirements
- **Normal Text:** 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratio
- **Large Text (18pt+):** 3:1 minimum contrast ratio
- **Interactive Elements:** Clear visual distinction from non-interactive
- **Focus States:** Highly visible, minimum 3:1 contrast

## Color Usage
- Don't rely solely on color to convey information
- Provide additional visual cues (icons, text, patterns)
- Test for color blindness accessibility
- Ensure sufficient contrast in both light and dark modes

## Semantic Colors
- **Success:** Green tones
- **Warning:** Yellow/orange tones
- **Error:** Red tones
- **Info:** Blue tones
- Use consistently across the interface
